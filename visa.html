Document Requirements

General documents required. Specific requirements vary by country.

Passport Documents

Original passport (min. 6 months validity)
Passport copies — all pages
Old passports (if any)
2 recent passport-size photos (white background)

Identity & Address Proof

Aadhaar Card (front & back)
PAN Card
Voter ID / Driving Licence
Utility bill for address proof

Financial Documents

3–6 months bank statements
Bank balance certificate
Income Tax Returns (ITR)
Salary slips (3 months)

Travel Documents

Confirmed flight itinerary
Hotel booking confirmation
Travel insurance certificate
Tour itinerary / invitation letter

Employment Proof

Employment letter from employer
Leave approval letter
Business registration (self-employed)
NOC from employer

For Umrah / Hajj

Meningitis vaccination certificate
Mahram relationship proof (women)
Nikah nama / marriage certificate
Group leader approval letter
